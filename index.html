About Todd Kidd

Founder – TK Coaching
Holistic Performance & Wellness Coach
Former Olympian

Todd Kidd is a former Olympic boxer turned integrative health and performance coach with a mission to train, educate, and inspire people to become the best version of themselves. Drawing from over a decade of elite international competition—including 17 professional bouts and representing Australia at the 2008 Beijing Olympics—Todd combines lived experience with evidence-based methodologies to support individuals and teams in unlocking peak physical and mental performance.

After undergoing a total hip replacement at 33, Todd redirected his focus from high-impact sport to holistic wellness. He is now a certified CHEK Practitioner and Holistic Lifestyle Coach, specializing in biomechanical assessment, injury recovery, structural balance, and lifestyle transformation.

Mission

My mission is to train, educate, and inspire people to become the best version of themselves—minimizing injury risk, maximizing performance, and promoting sustainable health habits.
